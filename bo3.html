Gameplay
Call of Duty: Black Ops 3, or bo3, is a first person shooter. The player is equiped with a gun and other equipment.
Multiplayer
In multiplayer, players can choose from a wide varity of gamemodes with dozens of different maps. You can play gamemode like team death match or objective gamemodes like domination. All gamemodes have one thing in common - you fight against others.
Zombies
Zombies is a round based gamemode in which the play needs to work to survive as many round as possible. Each subsequent round increases in difficulty. The maps in this gamemode are very big and unique.
Campaign
Campaign is a story based gamemode. The player follows the story to complete objectives. In this campaign, players fight through an open level that supports up to 4 players.
