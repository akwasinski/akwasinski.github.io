Gameplay
Minecraft, the block game, is as simple as a game can get. There are two modes Singleplayer and Multiplayer. In both gamemodes, you spawn into an open world with infinite possibilities.
Singleplayer
Inside of the singleplayer gamemode, players spawn into an open world in which you can do anything. The objective is to survive the nights, build structures, and beat bosses. Inside of singleplayer, a player has infinite ways to play. This is the gamemode to let your creativity run wild.
Multiplayer
Inside of the multiplayer gamemode, players are able to join a server in which they play against other players. Minecraft has a very wide veriaty of servers that have a lot of different types of minigames, so there is a game for everyone to play.
