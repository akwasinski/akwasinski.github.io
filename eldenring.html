Gameplay
Elden Ring is an open world game with unlimited options. You are able to explore and world filled with enemies. The enemies in this game are natoriously known to be hard. In every area there are bosses in which the player needs to beat to keep progressing through the story. Each boss gives you a unique award to help the player.
Customizability
Elden Ring allows its players to completely control their character. Everything from the stats, armor, weapons, and attack style. As the player progress, they gain levels and stat points. These stat points can be used towards increasing strength, speed, magic, and so on.
Graphics
The graphics in Elden Ring is considered to be the best of the best. The game is able to completely immerse the player in the game because it looks so realistic. Even if your computer does not have good specs, you can still have a great experience while playing this game.
